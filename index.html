About Me

Passionate About Research & Innovation

I am a M.Sc. student in Computer Engineering (Software Engineering) at Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic), Iran. My research Interests include Machine Learning, Reinforcement, Cloud and Serverless Computing, Scheduling and Optimization.

My master's thesis, supervised by Dr. Kalbasi, addresses the cold start problem in Function-as-a-Service (FaaS) platforms through mathematical modeling, optimization techniques, and online/offline scheduling algorithms.

I am actively seeking PhD opportunities in Cloud Computing, Distributed Systems, Machine Learning, and Reinforcement Learning for systems optimization.
