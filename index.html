About Me

I am a first-rank M.Sc. student in Computer Engineering (Software Engineering) at Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic), Iran. My research focuses on developing energy-aware scheduling strategies for serverless computing platforms to enhance Quality of Service while optimizing resource consumption.

My master's thesis, supervised by Dr. Kalbasi, addresses the cold start problem in Function-as-a-Service (FaaS) platforms through mathematical modeling, optimization techniques, and online/offline scheduling algorithms.

Beyond my thesis work, I have conducted research analyzing the SPECpower-ssj2008 benchmark dataset spanning 958 servers from 2007-2024, applying statistical learning, clustering, and regression models to characterize server power-performance behavior.

I am actively seeking PhD opportunities in Cloud Computing, Distributed Systems, and Machine Learning for systems optimization.
